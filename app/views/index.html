NHS login is a quick and very secure way for users to prove their identity online. It’s a safe way to make sure you’re giving the right people access to the confidential health information that your service provides without having to build or maintain your own digital identity check. NHS login is already being successfully used within the NHS App and is currently being offered to a wider audience of health services. Is the NHS login right for your service?

Online services such as health websites and apps are at risk of identity fraud and sensitive and confidential medical information falling into the wrong hands. Using NHS login to prove your users’ identity online can:

  • help protect your website or app from identity fraud
  • replace face-to-face identity checks
  • reduce the need for users to send documents in the post

Find out if NHS login is right for you and when it is not right for you.

How NHS login works

When logging into your website or app for the first time, users will be asked to set up an NHS login (username and password) using their email address and mobile phone. Users then have to go through a one-off step to prove their identity. To do this they:

  • upload a copy of a photo ID document, such as a passport or driving license
  • upload a short video of themselves reading 4 randomly generated numbers out loud

After the user has proved their identity, your service will be sent their NHS number, surname, and date of birth. You can also request other items, such as email address, which the user must consent to sharing with your service.
The user can use the account they created with NHS login to:

  • sign back in to your service at any time
  • access other health and care websites and apps that use NHS login

If the user has already set up an NHS login, for example to use the NHS App, they can reuse the same username and password. Read more about how NHS login works.

NHS login safety and security

Proving users’ identities using the NHS login meets the published standard for identity checking in health and care services. Read about the standards on proving identity that NHS login follows.

NHS login stores and processes users’ personal information securely and in line with strict standards.Read more about data storage.

NHS login support

NHS login is supported by the people who built it. The NHS login team gives your service:

  • 24/7 access to support
  • detailed technical guidance to help you connect
  • 99.9% service uptime (is this right)

Contact the NHS login support team if you need help or want to give feedback.

Tried and trusted

You do not need to build your own digital identity check from scratch or go through a long procurement process. NHS login has been designed and tested by NHS Digital and it’s already being used by the NHS App. Many other health websites and apps are going through the process of being connected to NHS login or have requested connection.
