To on-board with us you’ll need to have completed your Data Security and Protection Toolkit (DSPT). This is an online self assessment tool that allows organisations to measure their performance against the National Data Guardian’s 10 data security standards. All organisations that have access to NHS patient data and systems must use this toolkit to provide assurance that they are practising good data security and that personal information is handled correctly.
